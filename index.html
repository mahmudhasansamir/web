Completing Sentence

Some Notes:-

  • Sub এর পর V2থাকলে past Indifinite হয়।
  • Conditional sentense এ প্রথম অংশের পরের কমা(,) দিয়ে তাহলে বোঝায়।
  • sub + had + V3 থাকলে Past Perfect হয়।
  • Auxiliary Verb দারা শুরু হওয়া কোন sentense এ যদি Qustion Mark না থাকে তাহলে তা Conditional Sentence এবং Present Tense থাকলে 1st Conditional ; Past Indifinite থাকলে 2nd Conditional; Past Perfect Tense থাকলে 3rd Conditional হয়।
  • If/Unless/As if/As though/Wish/Would that ইত্যাদির পর Past Indifinite tense এ was এর পরিবর্তে were বসে।

Rules:-

  • Conditional Sentence হলে নিচের নিয়মে বাক্য গঠিত হবেঃ
    • 1st Conditional: If/Unless + Present Tense + sub + can/may/will + v1+O.W.
    • 2nd Conditional: If/Unless + Past Indifinite + sub + could/maight/would + v1+O.W.
    • 3rd Conditional: If/Unless + Past Perfect Tense + sub + could/maight/would + have v3+O.W.
      • [ বিঃদ্রঃ Conditional sentence এ auxiliary verb এর পর প্রয়োজন হলে not ব্যবহার করা যায় ]

    Example:

    • If the man came to me, I would help him.
    • Unless she had helped the boy, he would have fallen in danger
    • If I have the oppertunity,I will use this.
    • Were I a bird, I would fly in the sky
    • Had I a lot of money,I would help the poor people.

  • Since এর আগে সময় উল্লেখ থাকলে নিচের নিয়মে বাক্য গঠিত হবেঃ-
    • Present tense + since + past indifinite
    • Past Indifinite + since + past p.p

    Example:

    • It was 10 years since we had met last.
    • 12 years have passed since I was in London.

  • It is High time(এখনই উপযুক্ত সময়) থাকলে Past Indifinite tense বসে এবং It was high time থাকলে Past Perfect বসে।
    [বিঃদ্রঃ High time এর পর To + V1 ও লেখা যায়]

    Example:

    • It is high time we changed our bad habit.
    • It was high time you had played football
    • It is high time to visit Cox's Bazar.

  • No sooner had থাকলে than + Past Indifinite tense বসে এবং Scarcely/Hardly থাকলে when + Past perfect tense বসে।
    • No sooner had I reached the station then the train left.
    • Scarcely had you attented the programme when it begin.

  • As if/As though (যেন/এমনভাবে যেন) থাকলে নিচের নিয়মে বাক্য গঠিত হবেঃ-
    • Present Tense + As if / As though + past indifinite.
    • Past indifinite + As if / As though + past perfect.

    Example:

    • The girls speaks as if she were a queen.
    • He pretended as though he had known everything.
    • She behaved as though he had not known anything.

  • Too + adjective থাকলে adjective পর to + V1 + O.W. বসে।

    Example:

    • He was too weak to move.
    • I am too hungry to tolerate.
    • The load was to heavy to carry it.

  • So + adjective থাকলে adjective এর পর that + sub + can't/couldn't/mayn't/mightn't + V1 + O.W. বসে।

    Example:

    • The girl was so weak that she can't walk.
    • I was so poor that i couldn't buy a car.

  • To/Inorder to এর পরে V1বসে এবং With a view to/Look forward to এর পরে V1+ing বসে।

    Example:

    • I came to him to to take (নিতে) a book from him.
    • I am looking for a job with a view to maintaing my family.

  • So that/in order that এরপর sub + can/could + V1 + O.W বসে।

    Example:

    • He worked hard so that he could shine in life.
    • I study hard in order that I can make a good result .

  • To ব্যাতীত সকল Preposition এর পর Verb এর সাথে ing যোগ হয়।

    Example:

    • Inspite of having a lot of money, he is a miser(কৃপন).
    • He took physics in lieu of(পরিবর্তে) taking chemistry.

  • While থাকলে নিচের নিয়মে বাক্য গঠিত হয়ঃ-
    • While + V1 + ing
    • While + past continuous
    • While + past indifinite
    [বিঃদ্রঃ While এর পর Subject থাকলে তাহলে অর্থ যখন আর Subject না থাকলে অর্থ সময়]

    Example:

    • I saw a snake while, I walking in the garden.
    • While cooking, I went to my mother.
    • While she was coming to me, I forbade her.

  • wish/would that/if এর পর Past indifinite tense হয়।

    Example:

    • I wish I were a king.
    • If I helped the poor.

  • Lest (নতুবা) এর পর Subject + should/might + V1 + O.W. বসে।

    Example:

    • He walks fast lest he should miss the train.
    • Check the beast in you lest you should be a beast

  • And/or/but/who/which/that(W.H. word)/though/although/till/untill/as/since/when/as soon as/as long as এর ক্ষেত্রে সাধারনত দুইটি clause হয় এবং clause দুইটি একই Tense এ লিখা ভালো।

    Example:

    • Though Bangladesh is a poor country, the people of this country live in peace.
    • Wait here until I come back.
    • Since the weather was cold, we didn't go out.
